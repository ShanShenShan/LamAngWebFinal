The Main Characters

Lam-Ang

Lam-ang is a legendary hero of unparalleled strength and wisdom, gifted with extraordinary abilities from birth. Driven by a quest for vengeance and a heart full of love, he battles formidable enemies and overcomes incredible challenges.

Mahiwagang Aso

Lam-ang's loyal dog is no ordinary companion. Possessing mystical powers, this faithful ally aids Lam-ang in his epic adventures. With a keen sense of danger and the ability to perform miraculous feats, the magical dog plays a crucial role in Lam-ang's quests, showcasing unwavering loyalty and supernatural prowess.

Mahiwagang Tandang

Lam-ang's magical rooster is a remarkable companion with extraordinary abilities. This enchanted bird not only serves as a symbol of good fortune but also plays a pivotal role in Lam-ang's adventures. With its supernatural powers, the rooster can perform incredible feats, helping Lam-ang overcome obstacles and achieve the impossible.

Ines

Ines Kannoyan is the enchanting maiden who captures Lam-ang's heart. Known for her unparalleled beauty and grace, she embodies the ideals of love and devotion. Despite the many challenges and suitors vying for her affection, Ines remains steadfast, ultimately becoming Lam-ang's beloved partner.

Synopsis

In a land of mysticism and valor, a hero unlike any other is born. From the moment Lam-ang opens his eyes, the world senses his extraordinary destiny. With the strength of a warrior and the wisdom of the ages, he grows swiftly, ready to face challenges beyond imagination. Prepare to immerse yourself in an RPG adventure like no other. Explore the mystical landscapes, engage in epic battles, and experience the rich tapestry of Ilocano folklore. "Biag ni Lam-ang" is not just a story; it's a journey of bravery, love, and rebirth.

About the Game

Embark on a story-driven RPG adventure inspired by the legendary tale of Lam-ang. Be your own hero, engage in dynamic combat, and form alliances with magical companions.
